About Us

Faces of Justice is a student-run blog dedicated to spreading awareness about the dangers of bias of facial recognition technology when in the hands of law enforcement. With our initiative, we aim to help minorities by encouraging the creation of facial recognition software that have the qualities of minorities in mind. These minorities include not only people of color, but also women, transgender, and nonbinary people, which more than half the population is comprised of!
